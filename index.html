About us

Glasgow's mechanic that actually turns up.

We started The Moving Mechanic because too many Glasgow drivers were losing whole days to garage waiting rooms for jobs that could be done on their own driveway. We cover the whole of Greater Glasgow and treat every callout the same way: turn up on time, diagnose it honestly, fix it properly.

Every job is done by an experienced technician who arrives with professional diagnostic kit, a full tool set and a range of common parts. No hidden markup, no upselling — a straight answer and a fair price before we touch anything.
